Soccer ball buying notes:
- For adults and players age 12+, size 5 is standard: circumference 68–70 cm (27–28 in), weight 410–450 g, pressure 0.6–1.1 atm (8.5–15.6 psi).
- For ages 8–12, size 4 is commonly used.
- For ages 3–7, size 3 is commonly used.
- Look for FIFA quality marks if you want match-level quality: FIFA QUALITY PRO, FIFA QUALITY, or FIFA BASIC.
- Choose material by use: synthetic leather/PU for better touch and durability; PVC is usually cheaper and more durable for casual use.
- For outdoor play, prioritize durability and water resistance; for indoor/futsal, choose the correct ball type rather than a standard outdoor ball.
- Inflate to the manufacturer’s recommended pressure and store dry to extend lifespan.
Sources checked: IFAB Law 2, FIFA standards pages, size guides from Adidas and U.S. Soccer learning resources.
