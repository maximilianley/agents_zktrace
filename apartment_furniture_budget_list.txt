Recommended starter items to look for under 30 EUR each:
1. Small side table / nightstand
2. Folding chair or simple chair
3. Small shelf or cube storage
4. Laundry basket or storage box
5. Desk lamp
6. Clothes rack or coat stand
7. Mirror
8. Kitchen stool or extra seating

10. Trash bin

Shopping strategy:
- Prefer used furniture for larger items
- Buy new only for small essentials or hygiene-sensitive items
- Check dimensions before purchase
- Keep a simple neutral style so pieces match easily
- Start with the highest-priority items: seating, storage, lighting
